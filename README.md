# Sales Dashboard (Excel)

![Dashboard](Screenshot (24).png)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes sales data from 2019–2020 using Excel to evaluate business performance across countries, categories, and managers. The dashboard highlights key revenue and profit trends to support data-driven decision-making.

## 📊 Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Charts

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 🌍 Country Performance
- Portugal, France, and Sweden are the top-performing countries in both revenue and profit
- Revenue and profit are highly concentrated in a few countries, with significant gaps between top and low performers
- Countries like Austria, Belgium, and Denmark contribute very low revenue and profit, indicating potential areas for improvement

### 📅 Year-wise Performance
- Sales and profit remained almost stable between 2019 and 2020
- No significant growth is observed, suggesting a need for strategies to increase revenue

### 👨‍💼 Manager Performance
- A few managers (such as Celine Tumasian and Othello Bowes) contribute a major portion of total sales
- Performance is uneven across managers, indicating dependency on top performers

### 🛍️ Category Performance
- Clothing, Games, and Appliances are the top revenue-generating categories
- Profit trends closely follow revenue trends, showing consistent performance across categories
- Categories like Accessories and Other contribute the least to overall business performance
